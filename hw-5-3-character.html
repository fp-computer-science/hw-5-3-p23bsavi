Monkey D. Luffy

Luffy starts off as a weak pirate and the clear protagonist of One piece.
His backstory shows that he is a devil fruit eater. This granted him a certain ability
From one of the devils of the sea. In return for the power, all eaters of a devil fruit
Lose the ablity to swim. Luffy ate the Gomu Gomu no Mi fruit, turning his body into rubber.
