Luffy's victory over Moria

Moria is the main antagonist in the Thriller Bark arc of One Piece.
At first his power remains unknown. But as Luffy and his crew begins to
Defeat more and more of Moria's underlings, it is revealed that he too ate
A devil fruit power. Moria ate the Kage Kage no Mi fruit which allowed him to
Take away people's shadows. Luffy defeats Moria by absorbing shadows of nearby
Survivors of Moria's tricks and becoming Nightmare Luffy.
